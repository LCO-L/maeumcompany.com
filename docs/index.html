Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MAEUM

What makes MAEUM unique? +

MAEUM runs entirely offline on your own hardware - no internet needed. Your data never leaves your premises. It works on basic computers that cost under $100, making AI accessible to billions who can't afford expensive hardware or cloud subscriptions.

What hardware do I need to run MAEUM? +

MAEUM can run on almost any device: a 10-year-old laptop, a $35 Raspberry Pi, even smartphones. No GPU required. If it can run a web browser, it can probably run MAEUM. Minimum requirements: 2GB RAM, 10GB storage.

Is my data really private? +

Yes, 100%. MAEUM runs entirely on your device or on-premise servers. No data is sent to the cloud, no telemetry, no analytics. You have complete control. It can even run in air-gapped environments with no internet connection at all.

How fast is MAEUM? +

MAEUM is optimized for efficiency. While it may not match the raw speed of data center GPUs, it's fast enough for real-time conversation and document processing. Plus, there's no network latency since everything runs locally. Most users find it faster than waiting for cloud API responses.

Can MAEUM handle my language? +

Yes! MAEUM supports over 100 languages including regional dialects and minority languages. From Swahili to Tamil, from Korean to Quechua, MAEUM speaks your language with cultural understanding.

What kind of support do you provide? +

We provide comprehensive support based on your plan: documentation and community support for startups, priority email/chat support for enterprises, and 24/7 dedicated support teams for government deployments. We also offer training, custom integration, and on-site assistance.

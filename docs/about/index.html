What We Do

Founded in 2025, MAEUM AI develops on-device AI solutions that run entirely on your infrastructure. Our technology enables government agencies and educational institutions to leverage advanced AI capabilities without exposing vulnerabilities in data security or relying on external cloud services.
We believe technology should serve humanity's greater good. Our primary focus is supporting government and educational institutions worldwide, starting with Education Fields and expanding globally to help build a better future through accessible AI technology.
